Research

My research activity centres around the security of mobile devices and issues at the interfaces between hardware and software. More specifically, the latter includes the following low-level topics:

  • Trusted execution environments (TEEs)
  • CPU and firmware security
  • Fault injection and side-channel attacks
  • Security issues and applications of device sensors
Some highlights include the discovery of design-level security vulnerabilities on all known sensor-enabled Android devices, developing mutually trusted channels for TEEs (see Intel's Academic Research page ), and
